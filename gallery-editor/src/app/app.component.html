Choose the wanted skin from the dropdown below.

To edit a group / card Title, click in the box, make the edit, and hit the 'Enter' key to push the change.

To edit the card attributes, click the "Expand Options" button, edit the wanted fields, and click the "Update" button to push the changes.
This will also push changes to the card Title.

To change the position of a group / card, drag and drop the group's / card's black handle to the wanted position.

There are two kinds of cloning:

  • Independent: The newly created item is it's own entity, with no link to other items.
  • Dependent: The newly created item is linked to the original and any change (except deleting) affects both items.

*Cloning a group links the items to the original ones. Click "Add New" or "Clone Independent Item" to make independent items.*
